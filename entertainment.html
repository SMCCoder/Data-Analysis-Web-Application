Entertainment

This entertainment section incorporates data and statistics about movies, video games, and the most popular songs.

Info

Begin by selecting the API of your choice which will retrieve data from the website indicated. Then select a visual and if desired, select a data analysis method. Upon selecting the Movie DB API, enter a movie name in the dropdown field. Options will load as you type and let you know if your movie is in the database. If your movie shows up, then click the 'plus' button to add the movie to your list, which will be your dataset of movies. Sorry, the Knoema API is still currently on its way. Then, click 'Get Data' and your selected data will display in the table. To view the visual you selected, select what you want to be on the x- and y-axis. Click 'Graph'.

Contact us for any other questions, comments, or concerns and be sure to share your favorite page on social media with the share buttons below!
